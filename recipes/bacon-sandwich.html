Aglio e Olio

Description

A classic dish, and a consistent late night meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Whatever you like)
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Twice as much garlic as you think you need

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to manufacturer's directions
  2. Fry garlic in oil
  3. Add pasta to oil with a few table spoons of pasta water
  4. Last step
